How to buy

Step one: Create a wallet

We recommend to download the Metamask wallet from the app store or google play. If you are a desktop user then download the Google Chrome extension through the official Metamask website. You can also try other Ethereum compatible wallets

Step TWO: GET ETH BALANCE

To be able to buy you will need ETH in your Metamask wallet to swap it for $MEOW tokens. You can buy ETH directly on Metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy it on an exchange and send it to your DeFi wallet.

Step Three: Connect to uniswap

To buy $MEOW, go to Uniswap’s official website and click “Connect Wallet.” Select Metamask (or your preferred wallet) and confirm the connection. Make sure you are on the Ethereum network, as $MEOW is an Ethereum-based token. Once connected, you’re ready to trade!

Step Four: SWAP ETH FOR MEOW

In Uniswap, select ETH as the currency to swap from and $MEOW as the token to receive. Enter the amount of ETH you’d like to swap, then review and confirm the details. After approving the transaction in Metamask, you’ll see $MEOW in your wallet shortly!
